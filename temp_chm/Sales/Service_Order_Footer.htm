Service Order Footer

The footer part of a service order has various components as explained below.

The total service order value appears in a grid at the bottom right corner (below Direct Entry grid) of the service order window.

The total service order value, discounts, sales tax (if configured to be printed) and their respective Net Values of the billing made are displayed in this grid.

Sales: The net value of the total service order is displayed.

Discounts: The total discount value, if applicable, is displayed.

Sales Tax: The total sales tax is displayed. Click the button beside Sales Tax in the grid and the Itemwise Sales Tax details pop up. These details display the tax calculation for each of the items in the service order.

Add Items: Click to make additional entries in the service order from the Direct Entry grid.

Total No. of Items: The total number of line items in the Item Details grid is displayed.  

Total Qty.: The total quantity of items selected for billing is displayed.

Sales Value: The total sale value of the selected items is displayed.

Item Level Discount: The total of item level discounts is displayed.

Total Tax: The total sales tax is displayed. The Total Tax is calculated as follows.

Total Tax = Total Sales Tax on items (both Tax inclusive items and Tax exclusive items)

Net Amount: The final amount to be paid by the customer is displayed. The Net Amount is calculated as follows.

Net Amount = (Total Service Order value +  Total Sales Tax) - (Total Discounts), where the Total Discounts is the sum of Total Item level discounts.

F7: To save a service order.

F8: To accept advance to settle service order by using different payment modes such as cash, credit card or cheque.

 

Note: After recalling a service order in billing, if you perform partial deletion, i.e., if you delete one or more items from the recalled service order, and bill for the rest of the items, then the status of the service order will be closed. Then the service order cannot be recalled again.
