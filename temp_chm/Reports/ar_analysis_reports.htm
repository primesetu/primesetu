Analysis Reports

Analysis reports facilitate analysing the business data easily. Tally.ERP 9 is used as front-end for the reports and hence, can utilise all features of Tally.ERP 9 reports, like explode and drill down (wherever relevant) each line in a summary report to view details. You can also export these reports to Excel and XML, and send through e-mail. Additionally, you can handle the report selection and configuration on the fly as well as print the report on any printer. Click here to learn more about the Tally.ERP 9 report features.

Analysis reports are designed using TDL and use SIS as the data source. This facilitates writing or customising reports without the knowledge of Shoper 9 database. This also gives the flexibility for getting the required reports prepared faster and easier.

Analysis reports are grouped into three:

  • Sales

  • Stock

  • Customer Offtake
