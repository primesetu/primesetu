GS1 Standard

GS1 is an international non-profit association with Member Organisations in many countries. GS1 is dedicated to the design and implementation of global standards to improve the efficiency and visibility of supply chains. The GS1 system of standards is the most widely used supply chain standards system in the world.

You can perform the following operations related to GS1 Standard stock number generation methodology.

Add: Click to define a new stock number pattern. Click here to learn more.

Edit: Click to edit the existing stock number patterns. Steps are the same as in the Add option.

Delete: Click to delete any of the unused stock number patterns. You can delete a pattern only if no items are added to the item master with stock numbers following the pattern. Once you click Delete, all used patterns are hidden. From the listed patterns, choose the patterns to be deleted by selecting the check box in the column Delete. Click Ok.

Exit: To close the window
