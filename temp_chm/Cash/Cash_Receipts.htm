Cash Receipts

Cash receipts at the counter are those other than the sales proceeds. This module allows you to record transactions like receiving cash from customers in advance for future purchases. Typically, advances received from customers (without a specific sales order) for reserving items fall into this category. Users should be aware that, though collected as an integral part of sales and service ordering modules, the advance collected is also stored as other cash receipts.

How to reach: Cash > Cash Receipts

The Cash Receipts window is displayed. In this window you can execute the following functions:

Add: To make a new cash receipt entry. Click here for more information.

Edit: To edit an existing cash receipt

Delete:To delete an existing cash receipt

Reprint:To reprint an existing cash receipt

Exit:To exit from the cash receipt window
