Pallet Slip

This option is used to bundle one or more Packing slip. You can generate Pallet Slip based on Customer, Showroom, Vendor, Sales Order and Dispatch Advice.

The pallet slip can be consumed in Billing and Goods Outwards transactions.

How to reach:  Stock > Packing > Pallet Slip

The Pallet Slip window is displayed. In this window, you can execute the following functions.

Add: Click to record a new Pallet Slip. Click here for more information.

Edit: Click to modify an existing Pallet Slip. Follow the steps as in the Add option to make necessary changes.

Delete: Click to delete an existing Pallet Slip.

Reprint: Click to reprint an existing Pallet Slip.

Exit: To close the window
