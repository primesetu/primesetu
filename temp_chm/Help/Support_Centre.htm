Support Centre

Support Centre enables you to directly post queries, through Tally.ERP 9, on the functional and technical aspects of all products from Tally Solutions. This displays all the queries reported by you through different modes, i.e., posted using Support Centre, e-mail, telephone, etc.

Support Centre provides:

  • Quick help and support: makes it easy and convenient to reach out to Tally Support and the Tally Service Partner, for support on any Tally product.

  • Access solutions to your problems any time: responses to the queries are accessible in the Support Centre. Availability of information on issues, interactions and resolutions reduces the need for repeated explanations on issues/support queries and thereby ensures quality help and support.

  • Track your issues on a real-time basis: track the status of your queries including the complete chain of interactions, irrespective of the mode of communication.

  • Access issues of any Location/Site: in the case of a Multi-site License, you can view the details pertaining to issues of any location/site.

  • Reach out to your nearest Service Partner: you can search for a service partner and post queries instantly.

You can access Support Centre from:

  • Shoper 9

  • Tally.ERP 9

In Support Centre, you can do the following:

  • Add a new issue/ query

  • Reply to an issue/ query

  • Close an issue/ query
