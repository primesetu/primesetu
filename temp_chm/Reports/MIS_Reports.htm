MIS Reports

The MIS reports available are:

  • Monthly Sales Comparison

  • Rate Variation

  • Sales Analysis

  • Attribute + Size wise Sales and Stock

  • Pending Transactions

  • Walk-in Details

  • Superclass-wise Sales/ Stock

  • Stock across Chain

  • Transaction Details with Image

  • Gross Margin

  • Bill Re-print

  • Sales Promotions

  • Salesperson-wise Discount Summary Report

  • Excise Report

Apart from the above reports, MIS facilitates a separate set of customer specific reports grouped under Customer Offtake.
