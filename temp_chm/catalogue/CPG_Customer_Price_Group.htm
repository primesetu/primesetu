Customer Price Group

This option is used to create price groups for customers, specifying payment terms, credit limit, etc. These price groups can be assigned to a customer using the Customer option under Catalogue.

How to reach: Catalogue > Customer Price Group

The Customer Price Groups window is displayed.

Note: Choose the required functionality by clicking the corresponding button located at the bottom of the screen.

Add: Click to record new customer price group details. Click here for more information.

Edit: Click to edit an existing customer price group details

Delete: Click to delete an existing customer price group details

Note: Apart from an additional field GO, the Edit and Delete options are similar to Add option. You can either enter the code of the customer price group required to be edited/ deleted or press F2 to browse and select one. Press Enter or click GO to display the details of the entered customer price group.

 

Exit: To quit the Customer Price Groups option.
