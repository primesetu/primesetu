Sales Order

Sales Orders are prepared prior to billing by the sales staff. The details of sales orders are recorded by this option. During billing, you can recall the sales orders that are recorded already.

The Sales Order in the main menu leads to a composite window that allows various operations related to a Sales Order.

How to reach: Sales > Sales Order

The SALES ORDER window is displayed.

New: To generate a new sales order

Edit: To edit a Sales Order

Void: To delete or cancel previously generated sales order

Reprint: To reprint a sales order document
