Carton Specification

This option is used to create different types of cartons in Shoper 9 POS. You can name the carton which would help in easy identification during packing slip generation. You can also define the dimensions and capacity of the cartons.

How to reach:  Stock > Packing > Carton Specification

The Carton Specification window is displayed. In this window, you can do the following operations.

Add: Click to create a new carton type. Click here for more information.

Code: Enter the carton code.

Description: Enter the description of the carton.

Capacity: Enter the number of items to be packed in the carton.

Dimension in Inches: Enter the Length, Breadth and Height (in Inches) of the carton.

Ok: To confirm and save

Cancel: To clear the details entered

Edit: Click to modify the existing carton type.

Delete: Click to delete the existing carton type.

Exit: To close the window
