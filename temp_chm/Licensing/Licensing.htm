Licensing

Software license is an agreement between the software manufacturer and the purchaser of the software about the usage rights of the software. Now a days, License is processed electronically, during installation.

The very simple and easy to use licensing mechanism helps to self administer all deployments, including surrendering and reactivation of licenses. When you have multiple licenses, Shoper 9 Licensing mechanism helps you to keep track of the various locations where you have installed Shoper 9.

Features of Licensing

  • Simple and user-friendly Licensing mechanism through online process.

  • Brings in the concept of Account for better management & administration of Licenses.

  • Central Management of multiple Licenses running at different sites in a secured environment.

  • You can manage your Account/ Site using the Control Centre on Tally Solutions website www.tallysolutions.com.

To understand these functionalities, the concepts used are explained here.

Every Shoper 9 customer gets an account. This account is called a Tally.NET Account ID (frequently referred to as Account ID). This is basically a short and easy to remember name for your organisation, for example, AcmeCorp.

Account Administrator: Every Account has one Account Administrator, recognised by an ID.

Single Site Account

An account with only one site (one serial number) is called a Single Site Account. For a single site account, your Account ID and Account Administrator's ID will be created based on the e-mail ID provided during the licensing process. For example, ram@mail.com. This will be treated as the Account ID as well as the Account Administrator’s ID.

Multi Site Account

An account with multiple sites (more than one serial number) is called a Multi Site Account.

If you have multiple sites, then each site will have a Site ID. This is basically how you refer to the site, for example, ABC Mall Bangalore or Retail Plaza Chennai. Every site will have one Site Administrator, recognised by an ID.

For a multi site account, your Account ID and Account Administrator's ID will be created based on your request. For example, AcmeCorp as the Account ID and admin@AcmeCorp.com as the Account Administrator’s ID.

You can choose and change the name you want to use as the Account ID but it has to be unique across all the customers of Tally. To ensure uniqueness, it is recommended that you use an e-mail id as the account id. Similarly, Account Administrator’s ID, Site ID and Site Administrator’s ID can also be changed.
