Franchisee A/C

Franchisee A/C submenu allows to record any franchisee and its corresponding Head Office details. It also allows you to record payment details made by each franchisee and necessary reports on outstanding amounts.

The franchisee A/C options available are:

  • Payments to HO

  • Franchisee Details Form

  • Franchisee Outstanding

  • Pay-in Slip Printing
