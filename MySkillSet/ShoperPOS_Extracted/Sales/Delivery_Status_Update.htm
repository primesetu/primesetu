Delivery Status Update

This menu provides information on the status of items that are to be delivered to the door of the customer. It provides information on pending, delivered, rescheduled and rejected deliveries.

How to reach: Sales > Delivery Management > Delivery Status Update

The Delivery Status Update window is displayed.

    

Expected Delivery Date: This menu provides the status update for a time period.

From Date: Select/enter the starting date of the period for which the status update is required.

To Date: Select/enter the end date of the period for which the status update is required.

Delivery Status: Select the required status of the delivery, Pending, Delivered, Rejected or Rescheduled.

The details of the trip sheet number created are displayed under Trip Sheet No.

Select the required trips sheet number.

Show: Click to display the details of the trip sheet status.

Pending: The status update for the pending delivery is displayed.

 

Delivered: Select to record the deliver the items

Delivered Qty: Enter the quantity value of items delivered.

Delivered Date: Enter the date of delivery of items.

Exchange- Item Received: Select this option if the customer has    handed the exchange item.

Delivered: The update for Delivered status is displayed.

Rejected: The update status for rejected delivery is displayed.

Rescheduled: The status update for rescheduled delivery is displayed.

Clear Filters: Click to clear the applied filters.

Settings: Click to configure the display details in the grid.

OK: To update the delivery status.

Cancel: To stop the update process.

Exit: To close the window.
