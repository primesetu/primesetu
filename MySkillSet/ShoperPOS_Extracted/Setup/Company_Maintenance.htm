Company Maintenance

This option is used to uninstall the specified company or Alter the name and address of the specified company in Shoper 9.

How to reach: Setup > Company Maintenance

The Login window is displayed to confirm the supervisor's login ID and password.

User ID: Enter supervisor’s login ID (Default is super).

Password: Enter supervisor’s password (Default is nothing).

 

Ok: To open the Company Maintenance window

Cancel: To quit the process

Uninstall Company

The Uninstall Company window is displayed.

 

Note: The currently logged in company cannot be un-installed.

Enter Company Details

Code: Enter the Shoper company code of the company you want to un-install

Name: Displays the company name of the company code entered

  

Caution: Un-installation is an irreversible process. The selected company and its data are removed permanently. The backup of the company must be taken before un-installing.

 

Ok: To confirm the entries made

Cancel: To cancel the entries made

Exit: To quit the option

 

Alter Company

The Alter Company window is displayed.

Enter Company Details

Code: Enter the Shoper company code of the company you want to alter

Name: Displays the company name, you can alter the company name

Address: Displays the company address, you can alter the company address

Ok: To save the changes made

Cancel: To cancel the entries made

Exit: To quit the option
