Salesperson-wise Discount Report

The Salesperson-wise discount summary report shows the details of percentage/ amount discount at item level or bill level given to customers. Report can be generated in three different formats — salesperson-wise summary, bill-wise summary and bill-wise detailed.

How to reach: Reports > MIS > Salesperson-wise Discount

The Salesperson-wise Discount Report window is displayed.

Date Range: By default, the From Date and To Date are the Shoper date. You can select any date range for generating the report. However, the To Date cannot be greater than the Shoper date.

Report Breakup:

Salesperson-wise: To view the summary report grouped on salesperson name.

Salesperson Selection: Click All or Range to include details of sales made by the corresponding salesmen. If you have chosen Range, select the salesperson details from the list.

Bill-wise Summary: To view the detailed report with individual bill details against each salesperson.

Bill-wise Detailed: To view the detailed report sorted on bill number along with item details and the salesperson name.

View: To view the report

Print: To print a hard copy of the report

Exit: To close the window

 

Note: In the report:

· Regular Discount stands for percentage or amount discount given at item level or bill level.

· Discount % on Discounted Amount = (Discount Given/ (Item Value After Applying Schemes/Line Level Discount))*100

· Values exclude tax and discounts, and hence will not tally with those in other reports.
