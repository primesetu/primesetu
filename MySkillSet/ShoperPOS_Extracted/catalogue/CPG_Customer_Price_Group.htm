Customer Price Group

This option is used to create price groups for customers, specifying payment terms, credit limit, etc. These price groups can be assigned to customers while cataloguing customer details.

How to reach: Catalogue > Customer > Customer Price Group

The Customer Price Group window is displayed. In this window you can execute the following functions.

Add: Click to record new customer price group details. Click here for more information.

Edit: Click to edit an existing customer price group details. Enter code or press F2 and select a customer price group and click Ok to display the corresponding details. Make necessary changes as explained in add option. Click Ok.

Delete: Click to delete an existing customer price group details. Enter code or press F2 and select a customer price group and click Ok to display the corresponding details. Click Ok to confirm deletion.

 

Exit: To quit the Customer Price Groups option.
