Sales Advice Slips

Sales Advice Slip is like an in-store sales order prepared by the sales staff attending to the customers. The Sales Advice Slips are useful in a retail environment where the following criteria are met:

  • Sales staff attend to the customers on each individual sale

  • Billing counter does not handle merchandise

  • Sold merchandise are handed over to the customer from a separate dispatch counter

A Sales Advice Slip is prepared by the sales staff for the items selected by the customer. Normally, these slips are generated in triplicate (three copies). The first two copies of the slip are given to the customer and the third copy is sent along with the items bought by the customer to the dispatch counter. The customer produces the slips to the billing counter wherein the bill is prepared by recalling the items already recorded in the slips. On settlement of the bill, the customer is given a copy of the slip stamped as PAID along with the bill. The customer produces the slip with the PAID stamp at the dispatch counter and collects the merchandise.

The Sales Advice Slips in the main menu leads to a window that allows various operations related to a Sales Advice Slip.

How to reach: Sales > Sales Advice Slips

The SALES ADVICE SLIP window is displayed.

New: To generate a new sales advice slip

Edit: To edit a sales advice slip

Void: To delete or cancel a previously generated sales advice slip

Reprint: To reprint a sales advice slip
