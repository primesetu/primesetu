Credit Sale

The Credit Sale report provides details of credit sales. Reports for outstanding against credit sales, collection against credit sales, as well as the credit note status can be viewed using the Credit Sale Report.

How to reach: Reports > Cash > Credit Sale

The Credit Sale window is displayed

Date Range: Select the date range by clicking the drop down arrows against From and To

Customer Detail

Code: Click the browse button to select the customer code.

Name: The customer name is also displayed when the customer code is selected

Report

Outstanding: Select to display outstanding credit bill details including the net amount, the credit amount, the returned amount, the paid amount, and the pending amount.

Collection: Select to display collection details against credit sales, including the net amount, the credit amount, the returned amount, the paid amount, and the pending amount.

Credit Note Status: Select to display the status of credit notes, including the net amount, the credit amount, the returned amount, the paid amount, and the pending amount.
