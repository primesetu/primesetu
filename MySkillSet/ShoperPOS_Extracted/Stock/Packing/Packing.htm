Packing

The option Packing is introduced in Shoper 9 POS to facilitate easy packing of stock items into cartons by creating Packing Slip and also bundle cartons to Pallet using Pallet Slip according to your requirement.

You can generate Packing Slip based on Customer, Showroom, Vendor, Sales Order and Dispatch Advice.

The Packing Slip/ Pallet Slip can be consumed during Billing and Goods Outwards.

The options available in Packing are:

  • Carton Specification

  • Packing Slip

  • Pallet Slip
