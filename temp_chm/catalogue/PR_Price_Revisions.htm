Price Revisions

Price changes of items happen due to various reasons. This option is to change prices of all items or selected ones from a specific date. Price revision is executed as part of Day Begin program if Shoper date is less than Price Revision Effective from date or as part of loading program, if Shoper date is greater than Effective date. If a price revision is loaded at a later stage, the system will not update the old records.

You can also import the Price Revisions defined at HO. Text files called SHPR are used to send price revisions from HO. The file naming convention used is SHPR_SOURCECODE_DESTINATIONCODE_NNNNN.txt where SHPR is a fixed string, SOURCECODE is a three digit HO company code, DESTINATIONCODE is a three digit POS company code and NNNNN is a five digit serial number. This serial number is used at POS by the price revision loading program to check the status of the previous SHPR file before loading the next SHPR file. If previous file is not loaded, system shall generate a log for the unloaded files or generate an alert depending on whether the loading is automatic or manual.

Any price revision can either be an increase or decrease and can affect either Retail Price or Distributor Price or both.

How to reach: Catalogue > Define Price Revisions

The Price Revision window is displayed. In this window you can execute the following functions.

Add: Click to create a new price revision document. Click here for more information.

Edit: Click to modify an existing Price Revision. Enter the Doc No. and press Enter. The saved details are displayed. Make necessary changes as explained in the Add option and click Save.

Delete: Click to delete an existing Price Revision. Enter the Doc No. and press Enter. The saved details are displayed. Click OK and a confirmation message is displayed before deletion. On deletion, the price revision definition records will be displayed as cancelled in Price Revision Report.

View: Click to view the price revision report. Click here for more information.

Note: Editing and Deleting can be done only to the Price Revision which has not come into effect.

 

Note: If the price revision as per the document being deleted is already used, the transactions completed will not be reverted.

 

Cancel: To undo all selections made and select a new function from the Price Revision Catalogue window

Exit: To quit the Price Revision process
