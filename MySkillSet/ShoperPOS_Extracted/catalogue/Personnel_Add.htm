Adding Sales Personnel

The sales personnel window is as shown.

Official Details: The fields marked with * are mandatory.

Employee Code: Enter the code, as allocated by the company. This code is unique. You may press F2 to open the Browse window to check if the employee details already exist.

Name: Enter the official name of the employee.

Date of Joining:  Enter the date of joining. This can be a future date.

Type: The default available types are Person in charge and Sales man types. Select the type of the employee. The list shows the values catalogued in General Lookup catalogue, under the category Personnel Types.

Mobile: Enter the official mobile number, if any.

E-mail: Enter the official mail ID, if any.

Allow in Billing: Select to get the personnel details for selection in billing.

Personal Details

Communication Address: Enter the personal address, where communication can be sent.

Locality: Enter the locality.

City: Enter the city.

Pin: Enter the postal code.

State: Enter the state.

Country: Enter the country.

Residence Phone: Enter the residence phone number.

Date of Birth: Select the date of birth. This is optional. You may enter only day and month, if required.

Mobile: Enter the personal mobile number, if any.

E-mail: Enter the personal mail ID, if any.

Ok: To save the details entered

Cancel: To discard the details entered
