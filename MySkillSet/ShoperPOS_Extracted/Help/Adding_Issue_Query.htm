Adding Issue/Query

You can post your query for any support required on the functional and technical aspects of all Tally products.

To add a New Issue/ Query:

  1. Click N: New Issue provided in the buttons toolbar, or press Alt + N from the Support Centre.

  2. The form Post Your Support Query is displayed and fill in the details for each field. Click here to view the Post Your Support Query form.

  3. Send to: Select a New Service Partner or Tally Solutions Pvt Ltd. in this field. The Service Partner could be the one from whom you have procured Shoper 9 software or the one with whom you have entered into a service contract.

In case the user selects a New Service Partner, the recipient of the query/ issue will be a Service Partner. In case you select Tally Solutions Pvt Ltd., the recipient of the query/ issue will be the Tally Support (Customer Support Centre of Tally).

To choose a Service Partner, select New Service Partner from the list entitled Send your query to list.

The Partner Search screen is displayed. The Searching Methods list provides options to search by Name or Location of the partner. Click here to view the corresponding Partner Search screen.

Select the required Searching Methods option, enter the search criteria and press Enter. Support Centre will display a list of partners matching the given search criteria.

Note: In case of Remote Login, the Send to field will also display the E-mail address of the account into which you have logged in, under the Send your query to list.

In case, Tally Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is selected in Send to field, click here to view the corresponding Post Your Support Query form.

  1. Subject: Type the subject of query raised and press Enter or click A: Accept, to go to the next field.

  2. Description: Type the query description and press Enter or click A: Accept. The Post Your Support Query confirmation form is displayed. Click here to view the same.

  3. Send the query by pressing Accept? Yes or No.

The New query will be updated in the Support Centre query list as shown.

Note: In the process of sending a New Query subsequently, the Send to field provided in the Post Your Support Query form will list the partners chosen earlier in the Send your query to list.
