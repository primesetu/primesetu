Purchase Tax Register

The Purchase Tax Register provides purchase tax information for the selected period. The Summary option displays purchases associated with the corresponding tax type and value, while the Detail option includes information on purchase and tax at the bill-level.

How to reach: Reports > Stock > Tax Register

The Purchase Tax Register window is displayed.

From Date: Select the date from when the details are to be included in the report. The default date selected is the current Shoper date.

To Date: Select the date to when the details are to be included in the report. The default date selected is the current Shoper date. You can enter any date but it cannot be greater than the Shoper date.

Select the type of report to be generated, i.e., Summary or Detail.

Summary: To view only the summary of purchase tax.  

Detail: This report is at bill level. The bills are grouped based on tax rate.

View: To view the report

Exit: To close the window
