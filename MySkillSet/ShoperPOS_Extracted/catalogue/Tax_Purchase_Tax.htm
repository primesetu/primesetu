Purchase Tax Catalogue

This catalogue captures the purchase tax structure applicable for different category of goods purchased. The tax (or VAT) may differ across the items purchased. The taxes applicable are computed considering the rates and components defined in the tax catalogue.

How to reach: Catalogue > Tax > Purchase Tax

The Purchase Tax Catalogue window is displayed. In this window you can execute the following functions.

Add: Click to create a new purchase tax catalogue. Click here for more information.

Edit: Click to edit an existing purchase tax catalogue.

      1. Select the Source and/or Destination and the Product Tax Group as applicable.

The catalogued details are displayed in the remaining fields and the grid.

      1. Make necessary changes and click Ok.

      2. Click Cancel to choose a different function.

Delete: Click to delete an existing purchase tax catalogue.

      1. Follow step 1 as in Edit to display the catalogued purchase tax details.

      2. Click Ok to confirm deletion.

      3. Click Cancel to choose a different function.

List: Click to view the tax details already catalogued. The View All tab is activated.

      1. Follow step 1 as in Edit to display the catalogued purchase tax details.

      2. Click Print to preview and make a hard copy of the displayed catalogue.

      3. Click Clear to choose a different function.

Exit: To quit the Purchase Tax Catalogue
