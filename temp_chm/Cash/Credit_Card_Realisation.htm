Credit Card Realisation

This function allows the user to obtain the details of the credit card payments that have been submitted but not yet realised. If any amount due on a bill is realised, it is marked as Realised with the realisation amount.

How to reach: Cash > Credit Card Management > Realisation

The credit card realisation window is displayed.

In this window you can execute the following functions.

Add: To make a new credit card realisation entry

Reprint: To reprint an existing credit card realisation

Exit: To exit from the credit card realisation window

Agency Code: Enter a valid agency code of the credit card or press the F2 key and select the agency code from the agency code browser window. On entering the valid agency code, the details of all the submitted credit cards related to the selected agency code get automatically listed in the grid below.

Amount: Enter the total amount realised.

Agency Chq No: Enter the cheque number received from the selected agency for the realised amount.

Note: The caption of the field (for e.g., Agency Chq No.) is displayed according to the caption catalogued Payment Mode. Click here for more details.

Credit card submission details grid: This grid contains the details of all the submitted credit card payments, related to the selected agency code. The details are document type, document number, credit card name, credit card number, amount, commission and so on. The grid being displayed automatically, to mark any of the credit card details as Realized, the user only needs to select the corresponding check box under the column Realized.

Note: The realised amount entered in the Amount field at the top is equal to the sum of the amounts realised for the selected bills in the credit card details grid.

If the POS is a Company Showroom in a retail chain and in the Franchisee Details Form (under Cash > Franchisee A/C), if the option Agency Cheque Received in HO's Name for Credit Card Sales is selected, then the Payments to HO Bank Details window gets invoked. This facilitates direct accounting for the sales proceeds using credit card as the mode of payment, in the name of the head office. In such cases Payments to HO (under Cash > Franchisee A/C) will deal only with Cash sales.

Select the bank, type the location details of the bank, type the cheque number and select the cheque date (i.e., the date of issue of the cheque to the Head Office) in the respective fields.

Click Ok to save the details and it will give you an option to take a print out of the details entered.

Ok: To save the realisation detail, click Ok. Enter the cheque details in the Payments to HO Bank Details window.

Cancel: To undo all the changes made.
