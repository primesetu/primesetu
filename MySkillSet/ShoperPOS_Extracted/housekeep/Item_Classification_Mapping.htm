Item Classification Mapping

This option is useful only in the case of multi-brand outlets and is available only for the showrooms configured as multi-brand outlets.

A multi-brand outlet does business of products supplied by different vendors (principals), as non-exclusive outlets. Hence, such outlets communicate with multiple principals for receiving masters and sending transaction data of the corresponding items.

Data exchange between multi-brand outlets and different principals gives visibility of stock and sales across businesses. This enables the principal to track the movement of its products in non-exclusive outlets.

Multi-brand outlets use item classifications as per their preference. The principals interacting with such outlets follow item classifications as per their logic, as different businesses are involved.

In Shoper 9, smooth communication between such outlets and principals are facilitated using an attribute mapping to establish correlation. This option provides the facility for mapping the classifications followed in the multi-brand outlet to that of the corresponding principal. Apart from mapping the classifications, you can also map classification codes received from the principal to that of your choice.

How to reach:  Housekeeping > Synchronisation > Map Item Classification

The Item Classification Mapping window is as shown.

Note: You may reach this window through the Configure Synchronisation option too.

 

Principal Company: Select the name of the principal company. In case you are opening this window from Configure Synchronisation option, the name of the company to which communication is established is selected here.

Classifications:

Local: The field names and captions of item classifications in the showroom are displayed.

Principal Company: The field names and captions of item classification in the principal company are displayed. Select the required field or caption against each attribute in the local item classifications.

Map Codes: Click to map item classification codes received from the principal company to the required values of your choice. Click here to learn more.

Save: To save the modifications made to the item classifications

Clear: To cancel all the changes made

Delete: To delete the mapping of item classifications done against the selected principal company

Exit: To close the window
