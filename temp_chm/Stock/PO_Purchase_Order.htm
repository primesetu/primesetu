Purchase Order

Purchase Order option allows the flexibility to generate purchase orders based on your business requirements. Additionally, this option addresses the operations related to any purchased order which is already placed at POS or HO (if applicable).

The purchase order options available are:

  • Generation

  • Import

  • Foreclosure

  • Reopen

  • Export

  • Reprint

  • Status Report

  • PO/ Indent Conversion

  • Purchase Order Configuration
