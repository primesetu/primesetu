Packing Slip

This option is used to generate Packing Slip. You can generate packing slip based on Customer, Showroom, Vendor, Sales Order and Dispatch Advice.

You can also load item details for the generating packing slip from PDT (Text file).

The packing Slip can be consumed during Billing and Goods Outwards transactions.

How to reach:  Stock > Packing > Packing Slip

The Packing Slip window is displayed. In this window, you can execute the following functions.

Add: Click to record a new Packing Slip. Click here for more information.

Edit: Click to modify an existing Packing Slip. Follow the steps as in the Add option to make necessary changes.

Delete: Click to delete an existing Packing Slip.

Reprint: Click to reprint an existing Packing Slip.

Exit: To close the window
