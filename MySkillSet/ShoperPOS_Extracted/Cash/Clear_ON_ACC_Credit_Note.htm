Clear Credit Note

This screen is used to clear an On Account credit note.  

There are two ways in which an On Account credit note is generated. The first one is generated when goods are returned against a credit bill. In the second scenario, it is generated when goods are returned against a cash bill which is partially on credit. Here, one of the modes of payment in the bill is On Account.  

How to reach: Cash > Credit Sale Management > Clear Credit Note

The Credit Note Clearance window is displayed.

Prefix: The Document Prefix is displayed

No: The Document Number is displayed

The Shoper Open Date, which is the Document date is displayed on the right.

Credit Note

Prefix: Enter the prefix of the On Account Credit  Note

No: Enter the number of the On Account Credit Note

Note:

The Credit Note Prefix and Number can be got from the following screen:

Reports > Cash > Credit Sale. In the Credit Sale window, enter the required details, and select Credit Note Status under Reports. Then click View.

Show: Click to display the details of the On Account Credit Note.

The window displayed is as shown.

Doc. Prefix/No: The document prefix and number of the credit note is displayed

Doc. Date: The date on which the credit note was generated is displayed

Doc. Value: The value of the credit note is displayed

Reusable Value: The amount of the credit note which can be reused is displayed here.

Invoice Value: The actual invoice value is displayed.

Note:

The Reusable Value will be more than zero if payment has been collected against a Credit Bill or a Cash bill with On-Account Pay Mode, and it is returned.

Reusable Value will be 0 if no payment is collected.

 

Save: Click to save the details entered

Cancel: Click to clear the entries made

Exit: Click to exit from the Credit Note Clearance window
