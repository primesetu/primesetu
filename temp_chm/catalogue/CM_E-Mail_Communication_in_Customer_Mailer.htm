E-Mail Communication

Distribution List: Select the required distribution list of mail recipients

Field: Place the cursor at a required location in the body of the mail and select the field you want to print.

Add>>: Click to add the selected field where the cursor was placed. The value of this field for each of the mail recipient is replaced from the database in the mail sent.

Get Email Ids: Click to select the e-mail address of the all recipients available in the selected Distribution List. You may either opt to send the mail to all the customers of the list or send the mail selectively to the required customers.

To: Displays the first email address of the customer in the selected distribution list.

Bcc: Displays all e-mail addresses of the customers in the selected distribution list.

Subject: Enter the subject of the mail

Add Attachment: Click to add a file as attachment. A text box displays file name and the path from where the file is attached to the message.

Del Attachment: Select the attached document and click to delete. You may also select the attachment and press Delete key.

Send: To send the message to the customers whose mail IDs are included in the To and Bcc

Cancel: To clear the entries made

Exit: To quit the option
