Billing Configuration

Behaviour of a bill in Shoper9 POS can be configured based on business requirements. The following tables list the billing options configurable using parameters under the category Billing. The given table also contains parameters belonging to categories other than Billing, since some of the billing options are dependant on these parameters.

In the case of chain stores, HO decides the template for billing functionality and hence some of the parameters are being blocked from modification. This ensures consistency of billing functions across POS locations.

To add new customers during billing

1

The new customer insertions are never allowed during billing. Here, only those customer codes that are already catalogued can be used to select the customer.

To validate Stock availability of the selected items at different stages of billing

Stock Out Action in Billing

(This parameter is active only if the parameter Check Stock on Item Selection in Billing under the category Billing is enabled)

1 – Allow Billing With Warning

The stock validation takes place at the time of scanning the item. If the selected quantity of the item is not present, displays a warning message and allows you to proceed with billing.

2 – Disallow Billing

The stock validation takes place at the time of scanning the item. If the selected quantity of the item is not present, displays a warning message and does not allow you to select the item for billing.

3 – Stop Bill Update

The stock validation takes place at two levels. The first stock validation happens at the time of scanning the item and the second stock validation happens at the time of bill settlement. Here, if the selected quantity of the item is not present at the time of bill confirmation, displays a warning message and does not allow you to update the bill. The stock item details will be displayed in a different colour (Red) in the Item Details grid. This stock item details need to be deleted from the Item Details grid to proceed with billing.

To club repeated items in a bill

Club Duplicated Items in Bill

Enabled

The repeated stock items get clubbed together in the Item Details grid. Here, the same stock item selected twice are clubbed together only if the Rate, Disc Code, Disc % and Sales Staff are the same in both the cases.

Disabled

Repeated stock items will not be clubbed
