Defining a Purchase Tax Breakup

In the Purchase tax catalogue window, click Add Tax to add a tax division details. A new field appears to accept the tax percentage.

Tax %: Enter the purchase tax rate as handled by Shoper 9.

Ok: To enter details of tax divisions

Cancel: To cancel the tax rate entered

On clicking Ok, the Purchase Tax divisions window is displayed.

Tax Rate 1: Enter the basic tax rate.

Tax Rate 2: Enter the rate of first additional tax component.

Tax Type 2: Choose the type of first additional tax component. It can be Additional Tax or Cess.

Note: Fill in Tax Rates and Tax Types 3 to 5 similarly, if required.

 

Ok: To save the tax rate breakup

Cancel: To cancel the tax rate breakups applied

On clicking Ok, the purchase tax breakup details are displayed.

Add Tax: To add a new purchase tax breakup

Edit Tax: To modify the currently selected purchase tax breakup

Exit: To close the window
