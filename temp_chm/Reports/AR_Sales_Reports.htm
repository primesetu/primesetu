Sales Reports

How to reach: Reports > Analysis Reports > Sales

Bill-wise Sales Report

The Bill-wise Sales report provides sales information bill-wise for the selected period. You may also include cancelled bills, if required.

Product-wise Sales Report

The Product-wise Sales report provides sales information for the current date, based on Product Code & description information. It lists the Product that were sold or returned during the day selected along with their sales, return, net quantity and values.

Product + Brand-wise Sales Report

The Product + Brand-wise Sales report provides sales information for the current date, based on Product and Brand information. It lists the Product and Brand that were sold or returned during the day selected along with their sales, return, net quantity and values.

Item-wise Sales Report

The Item-wise Sales report provides sales information for the selected period, based on item information. It lists the items that were sold or returned during the period selected along with their sales, return, net quantity and values.

Monthly Sales Comparison

The Monthly Sales Comparison provides sales information of different months. It helps you to analyse the sales trend during different seasons of the year. A comparison report of sales in selected months of different years can be generated.
