Sales Factors

In releases earlier to Shoper 9 POS, this option was used to catalogue various discounting rules that were applicable for selected merchandise and specified duration.

Note: In Shoper 9 POS, discounts/ schemes are defined using Sales Promotion catalogue.

 

Other definitions that are catalogued in Sales Factors, irrespective of the version and release, are Retail Price Factor, Retail Price Round off, Add-ons, Deductions and Bill Round off.

How to reach: Catalogue > Sales Factors

The Sales Factors Catalogue window is displayed. In this window you can execute the following functions.

Add: Click to add a new sales factor. Click here for more information.

Edit: Click to modify an existing sales factor. Enter the Record No. to be modified. The details are displayed. Click Ok to confirm modification.

Delete: Click to select an existing sales factor for deletion. Enter the Record No. to be deleted. The details are displayed. Click Ok to confirm deletion.

Exit: To exit the sales factors catalogue option.
