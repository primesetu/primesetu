Update Customer Info.

This option allows you to attach Customer to an already confirmed bill.

How to reach: Sales > Update Customer Info.

The Update Customer Info. window is displayed.

 

Click to select the bill.

Document Details

Doc. Prefix: Displays the document prefix.

Doc. No.: Displays the document number.

Net Amount: Displays the document net value.

Customer Code: Press F2 to browse and select the Customer Code.

Customer Name: Displays the selected Customer Name.

Update: To accept the customer details.

Clear: To clear the selection made.

Exit: To exit the Customer Info. Update window without saving the details entered
