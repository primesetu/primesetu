Billing Hot Keys

Shoper 9 POS provides a number of Hot keys for the billing operations. The Billing Hot Keys List window can be opened either by clicking the button List Hotkeys in the toolbar or by pressing Alt+H from the billing window.

The Billing Hot Keys List window displayed.

Some of the frequently used Hot keys are listed below.

Key

Activity

F2

To activate Item Search window if the cursor is in Stock No field

To activate Customer Search window if the cursor is in Customer field

To activate Document Search window if the cursor is in Document No field

F3

To edit quantity

F6

To display various active discounts/ offers in the system and/ or enter bill level remarks. Behaviour of this window depends on the mode of sales promo selection in billing.

          •  Auto mode

          •  Manual mode

F7

To confirm and save the bill where the customer has tendered the exact amount by cash

F8

To make bill settlement by using different Payment modes

F9

To display the total values in the bill, i.e., the total of sales value, add-ons, deductions, discounts and sales tax at bill level

F10

To save the entries made towards payment

F11

To set focus to the Stock no. field to enter the items in the direct entry grid

F12

To suspend a bill during billing

Alt+C

To accept customer classification details during billing

Alt+H

To display the list of hot keys for billing operations

Alt+M

To change the customer at any stage of billing

Ctrl+D

To delete any item from the Item details grid. Here, you have to select the item by clicking and then press Ctrl+D when it is high lighted.

Ctrl+4

To make AddOns And Deductions
