Sales Tax

The Itemwise Sales Tax grid displays each item, selected in the item details grid, with its corresponding sales tax. In general, the items are classified into two types, namely; Tax inclusive items (i.e., the item rate is inclusive of sales tax) and Tax exclusive items (i.e., the item rate is exclusive of sales tax). Inclusion or exclusion of sales tax to the item rate is based on each item specified during the time of item entry into Item Master.

The Itemwise Sales Tax grid is displayed.

Itemwise Sales Tax Grid: The details like item types (Tax Inclusive or Tax Exclusive), stock number and the sales tax of each item are displayed.

Value: The sales tax of each item is displayed.

Note: The sales tax is calculated on the item rate (MRP, in the case of Tax inclusive items) after deducting discounts, if any.

The calculation of sales tax in the case of Tax inclusive and Tax exclusive items are as follows.

       1. In the case of Tax inclusive items:

           Tax value = (MRP - Discounts) * Tax percentage / (100 + Tax percentage)

       2. In the case of Tax exclusive items:

           Tax value = (Item Rate - Discounts) * Tax percentage / 100
