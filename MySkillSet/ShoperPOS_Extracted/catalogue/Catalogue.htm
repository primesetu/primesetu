Catalogue

Catalogues contain the master data and business rule definitions that are used by Shoper 9 POS.

Different catalogues that are available in Shoper 9 POS are:

  • General Lookup

  • Vendor

  • Item Classification

  • Size Management

  • Item Master

  • Define Price Revisions

  • Define Sales Promotions

  • Payment Mode

  • HO Chain Stores

  • Vendor Schemes

  • Market Operating Price

  • Serial No. Configuration

  • Taxes and Duties

  • Factors

  • Personnel

  • Customer

  • Define Multiple Prices

  • Listings

Apart from the above catalogues, you have an option, Listings to generate reports based on different catalogues defined at POS.
