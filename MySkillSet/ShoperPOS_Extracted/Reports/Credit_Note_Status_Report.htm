Credit Note Status Report

The Credit Note Pending Status report provides information on all credit notes generated, credit notes used in sales, and cash paid against the credit notes, for the selected period.

How to reach: Reports > Cash > Credit Note Status

The Credit Note window is displayed.

By default, the From Date and To Date are the current Shoper date. You can enter any date range but the To Date cannot be greater than the Shoper date.

View in spread sheet format: Select to view the report in a spreadsheet format.

View: To view the report

Exit: To close the window
