Shoper 9 POS Series A Release 1.31

Issues Resolved

Billing

  • In case of auto application of sales promotions in billing, schemes were not applied based on the priority.

This issue has been resolved.

  • When credit card and advance receipts are used for payment in a single bill, credit card transaction was not reflected in submission/realisation process.

This issue has been resolved.

Cash

  • After reprinting any advance receipt or cash payout, there was mismatch in cash collection details. Also, the document number in the reprint was different.

This issue has been resolved.
