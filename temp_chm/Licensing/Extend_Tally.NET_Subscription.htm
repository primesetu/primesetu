Extend Tally.NET Subscription

Tally.NET is a framework which works in the background and provides a broad range of services using the Internet for Tally products. A valid Tally.NET Subscription is necessary for upgrading and updating Shoper 9.

This option is used to extend your Tally.NET Subscription.

You can find the Tally.NET Subscription expiry date (valid till) on Shoper 9 Info Panel. Fifteen days prior to expiry date, this information is displayed in red as a reminder that due action needs to be taken.

Note: You can extend the Tally.NET Subscription from: Shoper 9 application, Tally website or Tally Partner.

 

The Extend Tally.NET Subscription window is displayed.

Extend Tally.NET Subscription: To extend the Tally.NET subscription.

Proxy Configuration: To configure the proxy server to connect to the internet (if required).

List of License Server: To list the License Servers accessible on the network.

Continue: To continue the Tally.NET subscription extension process.

The Extend Tally.Net Subscription window is displayed as shown.

License Details: The Serial Number, Account ID and E-Mail ID of Site Administrator appear by default.

Billing Details:

Billing Name: Enter the name in which the invoice of the Tally.NET subscription has to be raised.

Address: Enter the billing address.

Country: Select the country from the list.

State: Select the state from the list.

City: Select the city from the list. If your city is not available select Others and enter the name of your city in the Other City field.

Postal Code: Enter the postal index number.

Period Details:

Period To Extend: Set to 1 Year by default.

Price: Based on the Shoper 9 edition activated, the rate of subscription appears in the field. For the current price list, refer to Tally website.

Payment Details:

Method Of Payment: Select the mode of payment as Credit Card or TallyCurrency. Click the following links to learn more about payments.

Credit Card

TallyCurrency

The Invoice cum Delivery Note appears. You may print and save it for future reference.

On successfully extending the Tally.NET Subscription, the new date appears on the Shoper 9 Info Panel.
