Variable Percentage/ Amount Discount on Bill

This promotion type is to define schemes that can be applied as variable percentage/ amount discount on each bill with or without any additional criteria. The discount percentage/ amount as per the definition will be displayed during billing. You can alter the values at the time of billing, if required.

Discount Details

The Discount Details section is as shown.

In terms of: Select Percentage, Amount or Both to specify how much discount is to be given. If you select Percentage, the Amount column in the grid will be disabled and vice-versa. At the time of billing, the discount value as per the specified percentage or amount will be calculated and you would get the flexibility to change the discount percentage or amount respectively.

In case you have selected Both, you may either enter the discount as a percentage or as an amount during the definition. At the time of billing, the discount value as per the specified percentage or amount will be calculated and you would get the flexibility to change either the discount percentage or the discount amount.
