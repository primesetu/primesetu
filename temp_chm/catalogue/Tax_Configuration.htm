Tax Configuration

Behaviour of Tax Catalogue in Shoper 9 can be configured based on business requirements. The following are the parameters under the category Tax and the corresponding options configurable.

Product-wise Sales Tax Applicable: Enable/disable application of different tax rates based on the products.

Source-wise Tax Grouping Applicable: Enable/disable application of different tax rates based on the source of goods.

Destination-wise Tax Grouping Applicable: Enable/disable application of different tax rates based on the destination of goods.

All the parameters are Variable, and hence can be configured as per the requirement.
