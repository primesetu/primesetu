Item Master

This catalogue includes all the details pertaining to the stock items (SKU). This option addresses the need for recording/ editing the Item Master entries. Item Master entry can be done for an individual item or for a batch of items depending on your requirement.

How to reach: Catalogue > Item Master

The Item Master Entry window is displayed. In this window you can execute the following functions.

Add: Click to add new item details. Click here for more information.

Edit: Click to modify existing item details. Click here for more information.

Delete: Click to delete existing item details. Click here for more information.

Note: Availability of these functions varies based on the user rights.

 

Exit: To quit the Item Master Entry option.
