Buy Rental License

Rental License enables the user to rent Shoper 9 license by paying the requisite charges for a Month/Quarter/Year. The license can be activated and used from the first day.

The License can be extended or moved to Permanent License mode, at any point of time during or at the end of the rental period.

The Rental License also provides you the flexibility to change the product edition.

In Shoper 9 start-up screen select Manage License > Buy Rental License

The Buy Rental License window is as shown.

E-Mail ID of Site Administrator: Enter a valid e-mail ID.

Repeat (E-Mail ID of Site Administrator): Repeat the same e-mail ID.

Note: The Serial number and activation key will be sent to the Site Administrator’s e-mail ID given in the form. It is important to provide a valid e-mail ID.

Billing Details:

Billing Name: Enter the name in which the invoice of the rental license has to be raised.

Address: Enter the billing address.

Country, State and City: Select the country, state and city from the dropdown list for the respective fields.If your city is not available then select Others in the City field and enter the name of your city in Other City field.

Postal Code: Enter the PIN.

Product Details:

Edition: Select Silver (Single User), Gold (Multi User) or Diamond (Multi User) from the dropdown list.

Period Details:

Period to Buy: Enter the duration in numerals and select Months, Quarters or Years from the dropdown list.

Price: The price to be paid in Indian Rupees is displayed.

Payment Details:

Method of Payment: Select the mode of payment as Credit Card or Tally Currency.

Partner Name: Double click on New Partner.

Note: If you have Tally Currency Key then you can select Tally Currency as the mode of payment.

 

Submit: To buy rental license and open ICICI Bank Payment Gateway screen.
