Mode of Payment

The payment (tender) details are mandatory for any bill settlement. However, if the customer is paying the full amount by cash, then press F7 from the billing window to automatically record the full payment of exact bill value in local currency.

If the customer wants to settle a bill using different payment modes, including cash, the same can be recorded in the payment details grid, which pops up on pressing F8 from the billing window. For example, a bill of Rs. 5000/- can be settled by paying Rs. 2000/- by one credit card, Rs. 2500/- by another credit card and the balance by cash.

Note: On recalling a suspended bill with payment details entry, the details will be displayed in the Payment Details pop up.

On pressing F8 from billing window, the Mode of Payment/ Payment Details pops up in the billing window.

Mode of Payment: Shoper allows the following payment modes to be enabled. Select the required mode of payment.

  • Cash

  • Credit Card

  • Gift Coupons/ Vouchers

  • Cheque

  • Discount Card

  • Advance Payment

  • Credit Note

  • ON-ACC

Note: For any of the above payment modes to be available in billing, the same needs to be catalogued under Currency Catalogue. The payment mode, ON-ACC, is available only if a customer is selected during billing.

On selecting the mode of payment, the various corresponding codes catalogued for a particular mode of payment get listed in the adjacent field. Select the required code and the relevant details to be captured are displayed.

For Cash: Select the relevant currency code, if foreign currencies are defined, from the drop down list.

No. Of Units: The receivable amount is displayed by default.

Exchg. Rate: The exchange rate defined in the Currency Catalogue is displayed.

Amount: The amount [No. Of Units x Exchg.Rate] is displayed. You can alter the amount, if required. The amount entered in this field is considered as the Number of Units.

The payment details like Payment Mode, Payment Code, Number of Units, Exchange Rate, Amount and Description are displayed in the respective fields.

Total Qty: The total quantity/ number of items selected for billing is displayed.

Net Payable: The net amount that the customer has to pay is displayed.

Amount Receivable: The amount receivable is displayed. Initially, it shows the net bill value. As the tender details are recorded, only the balance payable is displayed.

Note: If you enter more than the receivable amount in the Amount field, the balance amount along with a minus sign is displayed in the Amount Receivable field. This facility is available only for Cash mode.

The payment details entered by Cash mode, where the amount entered is more than the receivable amount, is displayed.

Payment details for other modes: For other modes, the additional information to be captured are as defined in their respective catalogues.

For Credit Card: Select the required payment code from the drop down list.

Enter the Card Number and Authorisation Slip Number.

Amount: The receivable amount is displayed. You can alter the amount, if required. You cannot enter more than the receivable amount.

The payment details like Payment Mode, Payment Code, Amount and Description (Card Number) are displayed in the respective fields.

For Gift Coupon/Voucher: Select the required payment code from the drop down list.

Enter the Prefix/ Start Number and Number of Count.

The Number of Count entered is considered as the No. Of Units for Amount calculation.

Amount: The amount [No. Of Units x Exchg.Rate] in is displayed. You cannot alter the amount.

The payment details like Payment Mode, Payment Code, Units, Exchange Rate, Amount and Description (Prefix/ Start No./ No. of Count) are displayed in the respective fields.

If the payment of a bill of Rs. 175/- is settled by a gift coupon worth Rs. 200/-, zero is displayed in the Amount Receivable field. The balance amount is not calculated. The payment details are displayed as shown.

For Cheque: Select the required payment code from the drop down list.

Enter Cheque Number and Cheque Date.

Amount: The receivable amount is displayed. You can alter the amount, if required. You cannot enter more than the receivable amount.

The payment details like Payment Mode, Payment Code, Amount and Description (Cheque Number/ Cheque Date) are displayed in the respective fields.

For Advance Payment: Select the required payment code from the drop down list.

Enter Advance Receipt prefix and Advance Receipt number.

Amount: The Advance Receipt amount (the amount paid as advance) is displayed. You can alter the amount, if required. You cannot enter more than the Advance Receipt amount. Also, you cannot enter more than the receivable amount. Customer can be validated before selecting an Advance Receipt for a bill payment

Note: The same Advance Receipt can be used for the payment of more than one bill, till the total amount of the Advance Receipt gets exhausted. For example, if you have an Advance Receipt of Rs. 500/-, the same Advance Receipt can be used in different bill payments say B1, B2 and B3 of Rs. 100/-, Rs. 150 and Rs. 250/- respectively.

The payment details like Payment Mode, Payment Code, Amount and Description (Advance Receipt Prefix/ Number) are displayed in the respective fields.

For On-ACC: Select the required payment code from the drop down list.

Payment by ON-ACC is based on the pre-specified credit limit for each customer. The bill payment by this mode is possible only if the credit limit for the selected customer is same or above the Amount Receivable.

Amount: The receivable amount is displayed. You can alter the amount, if required. You cannot enter more than the credit limit. Also, you cannot enter more than the receivable amount. The amount entered is considered as the No. Of Units.

The payment details like Payment Mode, Payment Code, No. of Units and Amount are displayed in the respective fields.

Note: The credit limit specified for a customer can be used in more than one bill settlement for the same customer, till the credit limit gets exhausted.

For Credit Note: Select the required payment code from the drop down list.

The bill settlement by Credit Note varies according to the parameter setting, except in those bill settlements where the bill amount is same as the selected Credit Note amount. Click here to know how to set the respective parameters.

Enter the Credit Note Prefix and Credit Note number.

Amount: The amount in the selected Credit Note is displayed. You can alter the amount, if required. You cannot enter more than the Credit Note amount. Also, you cannot enter more than the receivable amount.

The payment details like Payment Mode, Payment Code, Amount and Description (Credit Note Prefix/ Credit Note Number) are displayed in the respective fields.

Note: The amount in a Credit Note can be used in more than one bill settlement, till the Credit Note amount gets exhausted.

 

The billing window with the payment details is displayed.

Note: The Payment Details grid displays all the payment modes along with the details, used for the bill settlement. You can delete all or any of the payments, if required.

To delete a payment detail entered, you can use any of the following methods:

   · Right click on the payment detail and select Delete Payment Details

   · Select the payment detail by clicking and then press F4 when it is high lighted

 

Note: Press Esc to add more items to the bill/ recall any document, after capturing payment details. You have a choice to either retain the payment details entered or delete it.

After retaining the payment details, you have two options to proceed with the bill settlement:

   · Press F8: The payment details entered previously get displayed and you can enter the payment details for the remaining bill amount.

   · Press F7: You are alerted with a message stating that payment details exists and you have a choice to replace the existing payment details with Cash.

 

Ok: To accept the entries made towards payment or press F10 to save. On saving a bill, you can print the same, if configured.

Cancel: To exit the payment details grid. You have a choice to either retain the payment details entered or delete it.
