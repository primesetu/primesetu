Credit Card Management

The Credit Card Management is one of the sub-modules of Shoper 9 Point-Of-Sale. This sub-module allows the following functions:

  • Credit Card Submission: This option allows recording the submission of the charge slips made during a period to the collection agency for realisation. Credit Card Submission finds its use primarily when manual charge slips are prepared and submitted to the collection agency with a submission form. The situations where EDC POS Terminals are available, submission is an automated process handled by these terminals. Accordingly, in Shoper 9 Point-Of-Sale, this functionality can be automated by enabling auto-submission while defining the credit card related business rules as explained under cataloguing. When auto-submission is enabled, the credit card tenders are treated as submitted as soon as they are recorded.

  • Credit Card Realisation: This option allows recording the realisation of money against charge slips submitted earlier. The Realisation process needs to be carried out always.
