Cash Payouts

Any cash transaction carried out from the outlet other than a sales transaction can be recorded using Cash Receipts and Cash Payouts.

Cash payouts can be used for making payments to the customers or for recording petty expenses with the reason code. Payouts can also be made against credit notes.

A cash payout occurs when a customer returns the goods purchased or exchanges them for items of a lesser value. He would then be issued a credit note, which can be encashed at the cash counter resulting in a cash payout.

How to reach: Cash > Cash Payouts

The Cash Payouts window is displayed. In this window you can execute the following functions.

Add: To make a new cash payout entry. Click here for more information.

Edit: To edit an existing cash payout

Delete:To delete an existing cash payout

Reprint:To reprint an existing cash payout

Exit:To exit from the cash payouts window
