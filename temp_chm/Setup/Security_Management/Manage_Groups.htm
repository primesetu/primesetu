Manage Groups

This option is used to add new groups, modify the details of existing groups.

How to reach: Setup > Supervisory Functions > Security Management

On clicking Manage Groups, the Security Management – Group Management window is displayed.

Add: Click to add new groups. Click here to learn more.

Edit: Click to modify the details of existing groups. Click here to learn more.

Cancel: To clear the selection/ entries

Exit: To close the Security Management window
