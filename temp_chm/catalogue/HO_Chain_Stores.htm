HO Chain Stores

This option is used to record details regarding Head Office and chain stores. The option to allow or restrict Goods Inwards and Outwards from the location can be specified in this window.

How to reach: Catalogue > HO Chain Stores

The H.O/Stores Catalogue window is displayed.

 

Note: Choose the required functionality by clicking the corresponding button located at the bottom of the screen.

Add: Click to record new details. Click here for more information.

Edit: Click to edit existing details. Enter the H.O/Store Code. Enter the code/ name or press F2 and select from the list displayed. Make necessary changes as explained in Add option. Click Ok to confirm modification.

Delete: Click to delete existing details. Enter the H.O/Store Code. Enter the code/ name or press F2 and select from the list displayed. Click Ok to delete the details.

Form: Click to accept/ enter details.

Grid: Click to display/ list the HO/ Store. In case you want to edit/ delete, select the required Code/ Name combo and then click Edit or Delete button. The catalogued information will be displayed in the form.

The navigation buttons help in viewing the records in sequence, in both, Form and Grid window. In Add, Edit and Delete modes, these buttons are not activated.

Click the relevant button to display First, Previous, Next and the Last record.

Exit: To quit this option.
