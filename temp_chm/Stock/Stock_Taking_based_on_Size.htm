Stock Taking based on Size

Size Grid Entry facility enables physical stock taking based on their sizes.  You can enter the size-wise physical count of the items for a given combination of Item Classification-1, Items Classification-2, Item Sub Classification-1 and Item Sub Classification-2 in a single line. This improves the stock taking productivity of the stores with large number of merchandise.

The Items grid can be filled in two ways:

  • Entering details into any of the first four columns manually

  • By pressing F2

Provide details of Item Classification-1, Item Classification-2, Item Sub Classification-1, Item Sub Classification-2 by way of entry or browse by pressing F2.

The size applicable to the combination of the item classification provided gets activated. Enter the quantity of stock available under each size. If there is more than one stock number available in the items master for any size, then a pop-up window StockNo Selection is displayed, listing the relevant stock numbers with check boxes.

Select the check box beside the required stock number. Click OK to proceed.
