Generating a new Excise Invoice

This option allows you to generate and print the Excise Invoice for the required transactions. While generating an Excise Invoice, you are allowed to select multiple documents from different transaction types, e.g., Sales, Transfer Out, Purchase Return, etc.

The Excise Invoice number assigned to a new Excise Invoice can be either generated automatically or accepted from the user, based on the settings of the parameter Excise Invoice Number Generation Mode.

The Excise Invoice window is displayed.

Common Values: Click to enter common values, if any, for the documents. This option helps to avoid entering the repetitive values again and again. Click here for more information.

The details specific to the documents are accepted from the data entry grid at the bottom section of the Excise Invoice window.

Transaction Type: Select the required transaction type from the list

Doc Prefix: Enter the document prefix

Doc No: Enter the document number

Excise Book No.: Select the Excise Book Number code from the list. The list contains the Excise Book Numbers that are catalogued in General Lookup. You can choose to leave this field Blank, if the Excise Invoice does not belong to any Excise Book. Click here to know how to define new Excise Book Numbers in General Lookup.

Removal Date: Specify the date of moving out of the goods from the showrrom/ godown

Removal Time: Specify the time of moving out of the goods from the showrrom/ godown

Vehicle No.: Enter the vehicle number used for transportation

Driver Details: Enter the driver details of the transporter

Note: The document details entered from the data entry grid are displayed in the Excise Invoice grid. While generating an Excise Invoice, separate Excise Invoice numbers are assigned to each document in the grid.

 

Ok: To generate the Excise Invoice. On clicking Ok, you have an option to print the details of the Excise Invoice generated.

Cancel: To clear the details entered.
