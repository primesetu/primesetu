Extension Framework

Overview

Shoper 9 is a popular retail solutions product that has been in the market for almost two decades. Over this period it has undergone major enhancements and revisions to cater to the ever growing requirements and demands of the customer. As the retail scenario becomes more and more competitive, retail solutions also need to be dynamic, accommodative and flexible. Keeping this in mind, now Shoper 9 POS has come up with the concept of Extension Framework that allows customised add-ons, without modifying the base product.

The Extension Framework in Shoper 9 POS helps easy deployment of customised add-ons (extensions) without disturbing the equilibrium. In short, Extension Framework in Shoper 9 POS allows extending the standard Shoper 9 POS functionalities to capture extra information/ data specific to each business scenario.

Features of Extension FW

The following are the prominent aspects provided by the Extension framework:

  • Extensions can decide whether the customised data is mandatory or not

  • The details captured for extensions can be displayed before confirming the transactions

  • Multiple extensions can be enabled at any transaction level

  • Extensions can be set active or inactive

Limitations of Extension FW

The following are some limitations of the framework:

  • Extensions cannot change the look and feel of the standard Shoper 9 application UI
