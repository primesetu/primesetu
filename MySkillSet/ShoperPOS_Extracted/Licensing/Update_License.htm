Update License

This option is used to update Shoper 9 license. This will enable Tally systems to maintain version details of your application. When further updates are released, a mail will be sent to you depending on the license details available with Tally systems.

The Manage License window with Update License option is displayed as shown.

Update License: To update the license.

Proxy Configuration: To configure the proxy server to connect to the internet (if required).

List of License Servers: To list the License Servers accessible on the network.

Continue: To continue the update process.

The authentication window is displayed as shown.

Your E-Mail ID: Enter the site/ account administrator E-Mail ID.

Your Tally.NET Password: Enter the Tally.NET password.   

OK: To update the license.
