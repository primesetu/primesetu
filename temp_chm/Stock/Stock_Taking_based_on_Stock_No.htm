Entry Type: Stock No.

To enter/scan the Stock Number, select Stock No. in the Entry Type field

During the Physical Stock Taking Process, provide the Stock No. by:

  1. Manually keying in the Stock No.

  2. By pressing F2 key

  3. Scanning the Stock No. on the barcode, with a scanner OR

  4. Importing the file containing the Stock No.

Click Import, to import the file containing the Stock No.

Press F2, to display Advance Item Browse Screen from which you can select the Stock No.
