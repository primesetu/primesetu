Sales Promotions Report

The Sales Promotions report gives you details of sales in the selected period, grouping it according to the sales promos that were active during the period.

Sales details with no promos applied are grouped with bills either with Bill Level promos or with Item level promos.

The Sales Promotions report can be generated on the basis of Bill Level Discount or Item Level Discount. Both the reports are arranged promo-wise. Additionally, the bills without any promos applied are grouped as follows:

  • In the Bill Level Discount Report — one line for all bills having the Promo Description No Bill Level Discount

  • In the Item Level Discount Report — one line for all bills having the Promo Description No Item Level Discount

The reports give the following details for the selected report period:

  • Number of days the promo was active

  • Gross sales value achieved using each promo

  • The item level/ bill level discounts given

  • Miscellaneous discount given

  • Add-ons and deductions applied to items sold under the promo

  • Net sales value achieved using each promo

  • The quantity of sales achieved for the items falling in the promo

  • The number of bills in which the promo is used during the selected period

Note:

In the Item Level Discount report, Miscellaneous Discount is the bill level discount, if any, appropriated against items sold under the Item Level promo.

In the Bill Level Discount report, Miscellaneous Discount is the item level discount, if any, against items sold under the Bill Level promo.

 

How to reach: Reports > MIS > Sales Promotions

The Sales Promotion Report window is displayed.

Date Range: By default, the From Date and To Date are the current Shoper date. You can enter any date range but the To Date cannot be greater than the Shoper date.

Report For: Select Item Level Discount or Bill Level Discount to specify the type of discount to be included in the report.

View: To display the report

Print: To make a hard copy of the report

Exit: To close the window
