Walk–In Entry Configuration

The parameters under the category Walkin allow you to configure different options related to Walk–In Entry form.

Walk-in Entry options

 

Parameter

Value

Effect

Walk-in duration

Walk-in – Start Time

Time (Start Time of Walk-in)

The entries in Walk-in form starts at the specified time

Walk-in – End Time

Time (End Time of Walk-in)

The entries in Walk-in form ends at the specified time

Walk-in intervals

Walk-in – Interval

Interval (mins)

The total Walk-in duration (between Walk-in Start Time and End Time) is divided into several intervals

Walk-in information

Walk-in – Extended Information

Configure the captions to be captured as the reasons for not making any purchase during the specified interval

The number of people who did not make any purchase due to each of the specified reasons can be entered
