Configuring Parameters to Prioritise the Calculation of Item/ Bill Level Discounts

The parameter Apply Bill Level Discount First under the category Billing gives you the flexibility to prioritise the calculation of item level and bill level discounts as per your requirement.

The calculation of the net amount of a bill varies based on the value set for this parameter.

An example to show the calculation of net amount of an item is given.

Suppose the item rate of stock no. 00001 is Rs. 1000/-, quantity is one, item level discount code applied is D1 and the variable discount at the item level is defined as 10%. In addition, the bill level discount given is Rs. 200/-.

The calculations are explained as follows.

  • If the parameter Apply Bill Level Discount First is set to 0: The item level discount is given priority over bill level discount while calculating the net amount.

Step 1: The item level discount amount is calculated and is deducted from the item rate.

i.e., the item level discount amount of the item = 1000 * 10 / 100 = 100

the total amount of the item at item level = 1000 - 100 = 900

Step 2: The bill level discount amount is deducted from the total item level amount of the item

i.e., the net amount of the item = 900 - 200 = 700

The net result of the calculations in step 1 and step 2 are as shown.

  • If the parameter Apply Bill Level Discount First is set to 1: The bill level discount is given priority over item level discount while calculating the net amount.

Step 1: The bill level discount amount is deducted from the item rate

i.e., the amount of the item after deducting the bill level discount = 1000 - 200 = 800

Note: If there are more than one item, the appropriated bill level discount amount is deducted from the item rate of each item.

Step 2: The item level discount amount is calculated on Rs. 800/-, which is the amount of the item after deducting the bill level discount

i.e., the item level discount amount of the item = 800 * 10 / 100 = 80

Step 3: The item level discount amount is deducted from the total bill level amount of the item

i.e., the net amount of the item = 800 - 80 = 720
