Reports

 

Shoper 9 POS facilitates an extensive range of reports that would meet almost all the requirements of a retail/ distributor business.

The reports available in Shoper 9 POS are classified into the following categories:

  • POS Reports: Various POS reports available are as listed. Click the required report for more details.

    • Sales

    • Stock

    • Stock Registers

    • Cash

    • MIS

  • Analysis Reports: These reports provide the flexibility to choose the display columns and pivot column. These reports use Tally.ERP 9 as front-end, thus bringing in the advantages of Tally platform capabilities to Shoper 9. You can create custom reports using TDL and avail all the advantages of export and print from Tally platform.

When you select an Analysis Reports from the menu, a report selection window is displayed. Click here to learn more about the analysis report selection window.

  • Dashboard Reports: These reports provide a visual summary of business data, view several key performance indicators at once, key changes in business metrics are instantly visible enabling you to make necessary changes to be made immediately where required.

When you select Dashboard from Shoper 9 main screen footer, several charts are displayed based on your configuration. Click here to know more about Dashboard Reports.
