The application of price revision records varies based on the parameter If Price Revisions are Applicable, Activate Immediately under the category Item Master.

Attributes of the Parameter

Value

Effect

Day Begin

1

Price revision details will get effected (applied) at the time of Day Begin

0

Price revision details will not get effected (applied) at the time of Day Begin

Price Revision Import

1

Price revision details will get effected (applied) at the time of price revision import

0

Price revision details will not get effected (applied) at the time of price revision import

Authorization

1

Price revision details will get effected (applied) at the time of authorisation

0

Price revision details will not get effected (applied) at the time of authorisation
