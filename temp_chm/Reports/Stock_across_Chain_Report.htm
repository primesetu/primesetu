Stock across Chain Report

If your showroom is a part of a chain store and you have ran out of stock for a given item, you can help the customers by guiding them to the nearby showroom that has the required stock. In the case of chain stores located in the same city or region, this is useful. To implement stock visibility across chain, the nearby showrooms need to be logically grouped together.

You can define Stock Visibility Groups in Shoper 9 HO based on the business requirements and make the showrooms in the chain part of these groups, at the time of showroom master creation. Shoper 9 POS user gets visibility of stock based on the group defined at Shoper 9 HO. To view this report at Shoper 9 POS, Shoper 9 HO needs to be online with a public IP address. However, POS locations can even use a dial-up connection.

Stock across Chain report is generated based on the data available at the head office, as per the last synchronisation. If AST is installed in Shoper 9 HO, showroom data will be fetched from replication database. Otherwise, it will be directly fetched from HO database.

The Stock across Chain report is used to view the stock of a particular item in any showroom, in the vicinity, belonging to the same chain, based on the Stock Visibility Group defined at Shoper 9 HO.

How to reach: Reports > MIS > Stock across Chain

The Stock Visibility Across Chain window is displayed.

 

General Selection: Press F2 against any of the fields, Stock No, Item Desc, Product (class1), Brand (class2), Style (subclass1), Shade (subclass2), Size, Shrm.Code, to display the corresponding list of values. Select the required value from the list. Once you select a value for any of these fields, Search would be enabled.

Click Contains to change the comparison operator, if required. Repeat until you get the required value. The operators available are Contains, Is, Starts With or End With.

Search: Click to display the corresponding item details and stock across chain in the grid.

Advanced: Click to view Advanced Selection options. This includes Rate, Department (super classification1), Buyer (super classification2), Fibre (analysis code1), Finish (analysis code2), Colour Base (analysis code3) , Styling (analysis code4), Usage (analysis code5) and Quantity.

Press F2 against any of the fields, other than Rate and Quantity, to display the corresponding list of values. Select the required value from the list. To change the comparison operator, click Contains. Repeat until you get the required value. The operators available are Contains, Is, Starts With or End With.

Enter the value for Rate/ Quantity, if required. To change the comparison operator, click Is. Repeat until you get the required value. The operators available are Is, Greater Than or Less Than.

Stock Details Across Chain: Displays a list of showrooms, in the group that have stock at hand. Details of showroom includes name, quantity available for the specified item and date of last replication of showroom data at HO. Click <<Previous to view the earlier page and Next>> for the consecutive page.

F3 or Alt + R: Change Criteria

F4 or Alt + S: Start Search

Alt + A: Advanced Options
