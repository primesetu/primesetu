Sales

Sales option deals with various sales related operations like billing of items, payment of bills, sales orders for items, and so on, in a retail environment.

The sales options available are:

  • Billing/ Return/ Cancellation

  • Sales Advice Slips

  • Service Order

  • Sales Order

  • Sales Order Conversion

  • Walk-in Entry

  • Change Payment Mode

  • Pending Order/ Trn Closure

  • Excise Invoice Generation

  • Transport Receipt Entry

  • Update Customer Info.

  • Remote Item Search

  • Item Identification

  • Trip Sheet Creation

  • Delivery Status Update

  • Resend Delivery Request

  • Item Tracker

  • Delivery Management
