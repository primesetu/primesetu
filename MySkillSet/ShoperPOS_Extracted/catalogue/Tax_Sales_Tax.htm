Sales Tax Catalogue

This catalogue captures the sales tax structure applicable for different category of goods sold. The tax (or VAT) may differ across the items sold. The taxes applicable are computed considering the rates and components defined in the tax catalogue.

Tax rates are specified for each combination of Product Tax Group and Customer Tax Type. The Customer Tax Type is to address the need for applying different sales tax while selling within or across states. These are specified at Customer Level and used only in Shoper Distributor, as all customers of retail outlets are considered local customers. The product tax groups and customer tax types are catalogued under General Lookup. In the Tax Catalogue, the various components and their respective rates are specified.

How to reach: Catalogue > Tax > Sales Tax

The Tax Catalogue window is displayed. In this window you can execute the following functions.

Add: Click to create a new  sales tax catalogue. Click here for more information.

Edit: Click to edit an existing sales tax catalogue.

      1. Select the Source and/or Destination and the Product Tax Group as applicable.

The catalogued details are displayed in the remaining fields and the grid.

      1. Make necessary changes and click Ok.

      2. Click Cancel to choose a different function.

Delete: Click to delete an existing sales tax catalogue.

      1. Follow step 1 as in Edit to display the catalogued sales tax details.

      2. Click Ok to confirm deletion.

      3. Click Cancel to choose a different function.

List: Click to view the tax details already catalogued. The View All tab is activated.

      1. Follow step 1 as in Edit to display the catalogued sales tax details.

      2. Click Print to preview and make a hard copy of the displayed catalogue.

      3. Click Clear to choose a different function.

Exit: To quit the Sales Tax catalogue
