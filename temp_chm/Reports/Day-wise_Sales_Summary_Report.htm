Day wise Sales Summary

The Day Wise Sales Summary provides a list of items (or a summarisation of items) that were sold or returned for the selected period. It prints quantity and value of sales, sales returns and net sales made using cash and credit cards as bill tenders, separately. The number of sales bills, returned bills and net bills made are also printed as additional information. Selection of Cash sales, Credit card sales, or both types of sales is possible.

How to reach: Reports > Sales > Day-wise Sales Summary

The Day Wise Sales Summary window is displayed.

Date Range: By default, the From and To dates are the current Shoper date. You can enter any date range but the To date cannot be greater than the Shoper date.

Bill Type: Select Cash, Credit or Both to include the corresponding bill types in the report.

View: To view the report

Exit: To close the window
