Additional Filters

Source: Select the company code of the source from where the price revision details are imported, for example, Head Office code.

Document No.: Select the price revision document number to be considered for authorisation.

Include Approved Details: Select, if approved price revision details are to be included.

Include Rejected Details: Select, if rejected price revision details are to be included.

Price Revision Authorisation

This option is used to authorise the price revisions that are imported from Head Office (HO). Price revision authorisation is useful for showrooms like franchisee stores or any other showroom which has the choice to accept or reject price revisions sent from their Head Office. Authorisation can be made automatic or manual based on the parameter setting.

The Price Revision Authorisation window is as shown.

Filter Criteria

The items for which price revision authorisation have to be done can be selected based on Stock Number or the basic item classifications namely Product, Brand, Style, Shade, Size or a combination of any of these item classifications. Check the box to the left of the field name and click All to select all values under the field or click Range and select the required values from the list. After selecting the Stock Number if you select any item classification, the stock number entered will be cleared and vice versa.

From Date and To Date: By default, the From Date and To Date are the current Shoper date. Choose the appropriate period. The price revision records with effective date falling in this period will be filtered for authorisation.

You can also specify additional filters to display the required price revision records by clicking  .

List matching items: To display the records those satisfy the criteria, in the grid.

Price Revision Details: The price revision records that satisfy the specified criteria are displayed in the grid. You may approve or reject any details as per requirement by selecting the respective check box.

Note: You can approve or reject any price revision record before it is applied. This can be done to price revision records created at the POS location too.

Approve: Select the price revision records to be approved.

Reject: Select the price revision records to be rejected.

Clear: To undo all selections made under Filter Criteria and specify new filters

Save: To save the changes made

Exit: To quit the option
