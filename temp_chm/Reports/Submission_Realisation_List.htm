Submission / Realisation List

The Submission/ Realisation List provides information on the recorded submissions/ realisations of charge slips made during credit card billing, through collection agencies, for a given period.

Submission is a process of sending a list of credit card sales information to the agency which has installed the EDC machine in the store. The agency, in turn, makes the payment, equivalent to the total credit card sales value printed in the report received from the store, periodically. Recording the payment received from the agency against the submitted credit card sales is called Realisation.

The Submission/ Realisation List report can be printed agency-wise or credit card-wise for a given period. The report can be printed for all agencies and credit cards or for a select few.

How to reach: Reports > Cash > Submission/ Realisation List

The Submission Realization List window is displayed.

Report Option: Select Submission or Realization to generate the report accordingly.

Date Range: By default, the From Date and To Date are the current Shoper date. You can enter any date range but the To Date cannot be greater than the Shoper date.

Select Agency Wise or Credit Card Wise to group the report accordingly.

Select Specific Agency or All Agency to include all agencies in the report or generate report for a particular agency. If you have selected Specific Agency, select the agency from the list.

Select Specific Card or All Card to generate the report for all cards or for a particular card. If you have selected Specific Card, select the card from the list.

View: To view the report

Exit: To close the window
