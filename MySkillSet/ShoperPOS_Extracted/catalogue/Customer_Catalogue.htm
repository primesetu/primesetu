Customer Catalogue

This option is used to create customer database. Once created, browsing can be done on various attributes of a customer like Name, Classifications, Profiles etc., during billing, to associate a sale to the required customer. This database is used in generating customer related reports like Customer Offtake reports.

How to reach: Catalogue > Customer > Customer Catalogue

The Customer Details window is displayed. In this window you can execute the following functions.

Note: The functions available to you depend on the rights given to you by the Shoper administrator.

Add: Click to record the details of a new customer. Click here for more information.

Edit: Click to edit an existing customer details. Enter the Customer Code or press F2 and select a customer  and click Ok to display the customer details. Make necessary changes as explained in Add option. Click Ok to save the changes.

Delete: Click to delete an existing customer details. Enter the Customer Code  or press F2 and select a customer and click Ok to display the customer details. Click Ok to confirm deletion.

If a customer has done some transactions, the customer details cannot be deleted.

View: Click to view an existing customer details. Enter the Customer Code or press F2 and select a customer and click Ok to display the customer details.

Exit: To quit the customer catalogue option
