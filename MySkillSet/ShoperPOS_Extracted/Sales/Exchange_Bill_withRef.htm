Exchange Bill

The Exchange Bill window allows you to carry out the bill for the items taken in exchange.

The Exchange Bill window is displayed.

The Exchange Value, i.e., the net value of the items being exchanged (in this case, Rs. 3335.50/-) is displayed on top of the billing window.

Balance Due: The balance amount, i.e., the difference between Exchange value and Net amount of the items selected in exchange, is displayed.

Note: You cannot abort an Exchange Bill, i.e., once you opt for exchange of items, you cannot come out of Exchange Bill until it is settled.

Press F8 to settle the Exchange Bill.

Settlement of Balance Amount, if any:

  • If the Exchange value is more than the Net amount of the items selected in exchange, you have a choice to keep a Credit Note for the balance amount as per the parameter setting. Click here to know how the balance amount is handled.

  • If the Exchange value is less than the Net amount of the items selected in exchange, the customer can settle the balance amount by any of the payment modes available.

Note: In case Batch, Grade or Location is enabled then the item details grid and direct entry grid would contain Batch, Grade and Location columns respectively. Click here to view the detail part of the billing window.
