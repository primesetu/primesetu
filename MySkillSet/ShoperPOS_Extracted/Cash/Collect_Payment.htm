Collect Payment

The Payment Collection screen is used for accepting payment against credit transactions, voiding an existing receipt, as well as for reprinting the receipt. Additional parameters may be configured in System Parameters under the category Credit Management. The related reports are available in Credit Sale under Cash Reports

How to reach: Cash > Credit Sale Management > Collect Payment

The Collect Payment window is displayed.

New: Click to generate a receipt to accept payment against credit sales

Void: Click to void the receipt made  

Reprint: Click to reprint an existing receipt

Prefix: The prefix of the receipt is displayed here

No: The Document Number of the receipt is displayed here. (Auto increment from last number)

Display Details:

Against: Select On Account or Payment on Delivery to display the invoice details of the selected customer.       

For: If On Account is selected, the display is based on Customer or Invoice. If Payment on Delivery is selected, the display is based on Customer or Invoice or Trip sheet.

Customer: Click the browse button or press F2 to select the Customer Code .You can also type in the code manually.

The Customer browse window is displayed.

Name: The name is automatically displayed when the Customer Code is selected Additional customer information is displayed next. The user can decide the details of the customer information to be displayed by setting the relevant parameters under Sysparam against the Category Credit Management

The selected customer details is displayed as shown.

 

Invoice Selection: Click the browse button or press F2 to select the invoice.

The Invoice Selection window is displayed.

The selected invoice details is displayed as shown.

 

Trip sheet: Click the browse button or press F2 to select the trip sheet.

The Trip Sheet selection window is displayed.

The selected trip sheet details is displayed as shown.

 

Show: This option is enabled only when the Customer is selected in For field. Select any one of the following values All, For Period, Opening Balance, Overdue or Overdue by 10day(s) from the drop down list.

The From and To drop down lists are enabled when For Period is selected in the Show field.

By default, the From and To are the current Shoper date. You can enter any date range but the To date cannot be greater than the Shoper date.

From: Select/enter the opening date to display the details in the grid.

To: Select/enter the closing date to display the details in the grid.

View: Click to display the invoice details.

PDT Import: This functionality can be used to import all the pending bill details, from a text file, against which you want to accept the payment. The text file should comprise of Bill prefix, Bill number and Bill date, separated by a  semi-colon. Click here for more details.

For example:S11;1;01-04-2011

S11;2;02-04-2011

S11;3;03-04-2011

S11;4;04-04-2011

Note: All the bills should be of one customer only, at a time.

Receivable Amount:

Actual: Select to collect the full amount for the displayed invoices. Once selected, this will automatically allocate the amount among the displayed invoices.

Minimum: Select to collect the minimum amount for the bills being displayed. Usually, this is half the actual amount. This can be configured by setting the relevant parameters under Sysparam against the category Credit Management.

The parameter Minimum Percentage on Actual Amount under the category Credit Management can be configured, as required.

Parameter

Setting

(The value used for the parameter)

Effect

Configure Minimum percentage on actual amount

Enter the minimum percentage of the total amount to be collected

For example, as shown in the above screen, if the actual amount is Rs. 7600/-, and the minimum percentage is 50%, the corresponding minimum value is Rs. 3800/-. This amount is displayed against Minimum.

The minimum percentage is calculated out of the total amount, and the amount will be displayed against Minimum

 

 

Others: Select to manually enter the amount to be collected

Note: If the receipt amount is more than the allocated amount, the user can assign the excess amount as Advance

Allocate Amount Automatically: This can be enabled only when the options selected are Minimum Amount or Others.

Minimum: Select to automatically display the minimum amount as configured in the System Parameter.

The parameter Minimum Percentage on Actual Amount under the category Credit Management can be configured, as required.

Parameter

Setting

(The value used for the parameter)

Effect

Configure Minimum percentage on actual amount

Enter the minimum percentage of the total amount to be collected

For example, as shown in the above screen, if the actual amount is Rs. 7600/-, and the minimum percentage is 50%, the corresponding minimum value is Rs. 3800/-. This amount is displayed against Minimum.

The minimum percentage is calculated out of the total amount, and the amount will be displayed against Minimum

 

Others: Enter the required amount to be paid on the invoice which will be displayed under Receipt Amount field in the grid.

Note: If Allocate Amount Automatically is not enabled, then the Receipt Amount has to be manually entered for each bill.

The Invoice Details are displayed.

Invoice Details when Customer and On Account are selected.

Billed At: The name of the store raising the bill is displayed.

Bill Prefix/Bill Number: The bill prefix and the bill number are displayed.

Bill Date: The date on which the bill was generated is displayed.

Bill Amount: The amount receivable on the invoice is displayed.

 Pending Amount: The unpaid amount of the bill is displayed.

Receipt Amount: The amount being collected is displayed.

Due Date: The date on which the bill is due is displayed.

Over Due Days: The period, in days, for which the bill is overdue, or crossed the due date, is displayed.

If the selections are On Account and Invoice, two extra fields, Customer Code and Customer Name with details are  displayed in addition to the above fields.

Customer Code: The code assigned to the customer is displayed.

Customer Name: The name of the customer is displayed.

If the selections are Payment on Delivery and Customer, the fields with details displayed are Billed At, Bill Prefix/Bill Number, Bill Date, Bill Amount, Pending Amount and Receipt Amount.

If the selections are Payment on Delivery and Invoice, two extra fields, Customer Code and Customer Name with details are displayed in addition to the above.

If the selections are Payment on Delivery and Trip Sheet, the fields with details displayed are Customer Code, Customer Name, Billed At, Bill Prefix/Bill Number, Bill Date, Bill Amount, Pending Amount, Receipt Amount, Exact Cash and Multiple Payment.

Remarks: Enter remarks about the particular collection details being entered.

The Collect Payment window with details is displayed.

Receipt Amount

 Advance: The excess amount received over the invoice value is displayed

Total: The value of the amount received is displayed

Note: The total of the Receipt Amount (Advance + Total) should be equal to the Receivable Amount selected.

Exact Cash: Select if the outstanding amount is collected in full by cash

Settlement: Select if more than one mode of payment, such as cash, credit card, discount coupons, and so on, are used to collect the receivable amount. This can be configured by setting the relevant parameters under Sysparam against the category Credit Management.

The parameter Acceptable Pay Mode Configuration under the category Credit Management can be configured, as required.

Parameter

Setting

(The value used for the parameter)

Effect

Acceptable Paymode Configuration

Enabled : 1

By selecting 1 for the selected pay mode, that mode of payment will be reflected in the fields under Multiple Pay Modes

Disabled : 0

By selecting 0 for the selected pay mode, that mode of payment will not be reflected in the fields under Multiple Pay Modes

 

Reset All: Select to reset all the details entered

Save: This option is enabled only when the collection is against Payment on Delivery or Trip Sheet.

Print Engine Configuration

Click here for more details on the Print Engine Configuration for Collect Payment

For configuring the print engine, do the following:

 Go to Setup > General > Print Engine Configuration

Click on Configure Specific Transaction

Select CREDIT SALE MANAGEMENT ->Collection Receipt

Under Printing Options, Click Turn ON

Under Configuration Settings, select Advanced Options

Click New

The Create New Configuration window opens

Under Specify Configuration Caption enter Credit Management

Under Renderer, select Credit Management

Click Add

The New Renderer Details window opens

Under Renderer Caption, enter Credit Management

Under Remarks, enter Credit Management

Under DLL Details Renderer DLL file path, browse and select the DLL SN501200.dll under Renderer DLL file path

Under Namespace enter value SN501200

Under ClassName enter clsrecvgenprint

Click Save
