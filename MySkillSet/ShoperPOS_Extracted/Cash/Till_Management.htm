Till Management

In a retail environment, Tills are used to handle any sales related operations like billing, return, cancellation, cash receipts, cash payouts, etc. In many businesses, Tills are recognised as POS Counters.

The introduction of Till management in Shoper 9 for the retail environment guarantees improved cash/ collection handling at Tills/ POS counters in stores.

Some of the advantages of having automated Till management system in a business are:

  • Provides complete control over cash management at Tills/ counters

  • Ensures secure and efficient management of cash

  • Allows reliable tracking of store activities handled at the Tills

  • Requires less staff to handle the Till management activities

  • Improves accuracy and efficiency of the operations

  • Ensures easy reconciliation of receipts and payments

In Shoper 9, Till Management functionality is introduced under Cash menu. Additionally, there are few parameters introduced under the category named Till Management under Setup > General > System Parameters to manage some of the necessary settings of Till related functions. The Till Management functionality in Shoper 9 can be accessed based on the parameter setting.

The Till Management options available are:

  • Till Status

  • Set Opening Balance

  • Cash Lift

  • Reconciliation

  • Open Cash Drawer

  • Till-Reprint

The reports on Till related activities are available under Cash reports.
